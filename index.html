About Zain Malik

Zain Malik is a dedicated student at the University of North Florida, pursuing a major in Information Science with a minor in International Business. As an Honors Student in the Hicks Honors College at UNF, Zain excels in his academic pursuits. He also brings his expertise to the Information Technology Services Help Desk on campus, where he provides essential support for the IT needs of UNF Staff and Students, resolving hardware, software, network, and application issues with precision and efficiency. Zain's professional experience also extends to a previous role as a Web Development Intern at Elyk Innovation. Enriched by an Exchange Semester at Sophia University in Tokyo, Japan, as part of his International Business minor, he has broadened his global perspective. He aspires to leverage this valuable experience towards achieving his ambition of becoming a Systems Analyst in an International Organization. Beyond academics and work, Zain actively participates in extracurricular activities such as Osprey Robotics and the Muslim Student Association, showcasing his well-rounded approach to personal and professional development.
